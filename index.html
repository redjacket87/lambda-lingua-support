Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select Lambda Lingua → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the paid period.

How do I restore a purchase on a new device?

Sign in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase. Open the app — Lambda Lingua restores the subscription automatically when it detects the active App Store entitlement.

What does Pro give me?
  • Unlimited custom reading texts (free plan: up to 3)
  • Up to 5 AI-generated stories and listening exercises per day (free: 1)
  • OCR camera scan
  • Smart card auto-fill, grammar explanations
  • 3000 DeepL translation characters per day (free: 500)
I redeemed a promo code but Pro is not active

Promo codes are processed by Apple. If Pro doesn't unlock within a few minutes, force-quit the app and open it again. Still no luck? Email us with the code and your Apple ID region.

Translation says "needs internet or iOS 18+"

Lambda Lingua uses DeepL for translation. If you're offline and on iOS 17 or earlier, on-device translation isn't available. Solutions: connect to Wi-Fi/cellular, or upgrade to iOS 18 (which enables Apple's offline Translation framework).
