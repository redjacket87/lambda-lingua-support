Privacy Policy

Lambda Lingua • Effective: 30 April 2026

This policy describes what data Lambda Lingua processes, where it goes, and how to contact us. The app is offered for iOS and Android.

1. What data we process

Stored on your device only

  • Your flashcards, custom reading texts, listening sessions, learning streak, and study progress — saved in a local database. Never uploaded to our servers.
  • App preferences (UI language, theme, notification time).
  • Subscription / purchase status (managed by Apple App Store or Google Play; we only read whether you have an active entitlement).

Sent to third-party AI / translation services on demand

  • Translation requests — when you tap a word for translation, the word and its sentence context may be sent to DeepL (deepl.com/privacy). On iOS 18+ Apple's on-device Translation framework is preferred and runs locally. On Android, Google ML Kit is used as offline fallback (on-device).
  • AI text generation — when you generate a reading text or listening exercise, your prompt and language preference are sent to Google Gemini (policies.google.com/privacy) or DeepSeek (deepseek.com/privacy_policy). The generated text is then stored locally on your device.
  • OCR (camera scan) — when you scan printed text, the image is sent to Google Gemini for recognition, then discarded. Recognised text is stored locally only if you save it.

We do not store these requests on our servers. We pay these providers for the API calls; they handle the data per their own privacy policies.

Permissions requested

  • Camera — only when you tap "Scan with camera" for OCR. Not used in the background.
  • Photos / Gallery — only when you pick an image for OCR.
  • Notifications — for the optional daily study reminder. You can turn it off in app settings or system settings.
  • Storage (Android) — only when you import a text file (.txt / .epub / .zip).

2. What we do not collect

  • No accounts, no sign-in.
  • No analytics SDKs, no crash reporting (e.g. Firebase, Mixpanel).
  • No advertising identifiers, no tracking pixels, no third-party ads.
  • No location data.

3. Subscriptions and payments

Pro subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). We never see your credit card or billing details. We only receive an "active subscription" signal from the respective platform. Cancellation, refund requests, and family sharing are managed in your App Store / Play Store account.

4. Data retention

Your local data lives on your device until you delete the app or reset its data. Removing the app removes everything stored locally. We do not retain user-specific server-side data because we do not store it server-side in the first place.

5. Children

Lambda Lingua is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the app and you would like to report a concern, contact us.

6. Your rights

Because we don't store personal data on our servers, there is nothing to access, export, or delete on the server side. To remove your local data, uninstall the app or use system "Clear app data" in Android settings.

7. Changes to this policy

If we change how the app processes data, we will publish an updated policy at this URL with a new "Effective" date. Material changes will also be announced inside the app.
